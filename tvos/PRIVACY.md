# Privacy Policy for VibeScapeTV

**Effective Date:** January 2, 2026

## Overview

VibeScapeTV ("the App") is developed by Jason A. Cox. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

**In short: We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.**

## Information We Do NOT Collect

- No personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- No usage analytics or tracking
- No crash reports or diagnostics sent to us
- No advertising identifiers
- No location data
- No device identifiers

## Local Data Storage

The App stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

- **Server URL**: The VibeScape server address you configure in Settings
- **Display Preferences**: Your prompt visibility preference

This data is stored using Apple's standard UserDefaults and:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not transmitted to us or any third party
- Is automatically deleted when you uninstall the App

## Network Communication

The App connects only to the VibeScape server URL that you specify. This connection:
- Is initiated solely by you
- Fetches images from your configured server
- Does not send any personal or device information

We have no visibility into your server communications.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. It is safe for users of all ages.

## Data Deletion

Since we don't collect any data, there is nothing to delete on our end. To remove all locally stored preferences:
- Simply delete the App from your Apple TV

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will post the new version in the App's repository and update the effective date above.
